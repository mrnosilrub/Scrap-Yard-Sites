Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 4 July 2025

Scrapyard Sites (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit scrapyardsites.com (the “Site”) or interact with any of our services, prototypes, or test sites hosted on GitHub or other platforms (collectively, the “Services”). By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Category What We Collect Source Purpose
Personal Information Name, business name, email address, phone number, and any other contact details you provide via forms, email, or live chat Directly from you Respond to inquiries, deliver proposals, provide customer support
Usage Data IP address, browser type, device identifiers, referring pages, pages visited, time spent, links clicked Automated through cookies or similar technologies Site analytics, security, service improvement
Project‑Related Content Logos, text, images, or other materials you upload or share for site‑building Directly from you Designing, developing, and showcasing test websites

No Sensitive Data: We do not intentionally collect government identifiers, financial account numbers, precise geolocation, or any “special category” data as defined by GDPR.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide & Improve Services – create and maintain websites, troubleshoot issues, and enhance user experience.
  • Communicate – respond to requests, send updates, and deliver essential service notices.
  • Marketing (No Spam) – send occasional newsletters or promotions only with your explicit consent; all marketing emails include an easy opt‑out link in compliance with the CAN‑SPAM Act.
  • Analytics & Security – analyze traffic patterns, detect and mitigate fraud or abuse, and ensure the integrity of our infrastructure.
  • Legal & Compliance – satisfy contractual or legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

3. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use first‑party cookies and comparable technologies (e.g., local storage, pixel tags) to:

  • Remember site preferences;
  • Measure traffic and usage patterns (via Google Analytics or a comparable privacy‑focused alternative);
  • Secure our Services.

You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may limit certain features.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only:

  • Service Providers – trusted vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, payments) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Business Transfers – in connection with a corporate merger, acquisition, or sale (you will be notified before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy).
  • Legal Requirements – to comply with a subpoena, court order, or other lawful request, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required by law (e.g., tax or accounting regulations). When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Jurisdiction Your Rights

7. Security

We use industry‑standard technical and organizational safeguards—including HTTPS, access controls, encryption at rest and in transit, regular vulnerability scanning, and least‑privilege principles—to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100 % secure; therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are intended for business users over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 years old. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe a child has provided us information may contact us at hello@scrapyardsites.com.

9. Third‑Party Links

Our Site and demo projects may contain links to external sites or embedded services (e.g., maps, social media widgets). This Privacy Policy does not govern third‑party privacy practices. We encourage you to review the policies of any external site you visit.

10. International Transfers

We are based in the United States. If you access our Services from outside the U.S., you consent to transferring your data to and processing it in the U.S. or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate, which may have different data‑protection laws.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the revised version with a new “Effective Date” and, where appropriate, provide additional notice (e.g., email or prominent banner). Continued use of the Services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
