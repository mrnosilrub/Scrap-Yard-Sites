Copper & Brass

We pay top dollar for:

  • #1 Bright & Shiny wire
  • Insulated copper cable
  • Yellow brass solids

We can’t accept:

  • Air-conditioning coils with freon
  • Greasy or food-contaminated copper

Aluminum

We pay top dollar for:

  • Extrusion and sheet
  • Cast wheels and blocks

We can’t accept:

  • Foil pans
  • Food-contaminated scrap

Steel & Iron

We pay top dollar for:

  • HMS and prepared plate
  • Cast iron machinery

We can’t accept:

  • Pressurized tanks
  • Paint cans

Stainless Steel

We pay top dollar for:

  • 304/316 solids
  • Food-grade tanks

We can’t accept:

  • Greasy restaurant scrap

Catalytic Converters

We pay top dollar for:

  • OEM units
  • DPF and foil cats

We can’t accept:

  • Empty or punched shells

E‑Scrap & Batteries

We pay top dollar for:

  • Server boards
  • Lead-acid & Li‑ion packs

We can’t accept:

  • CRT monitors
  • Alkaline batteries
