FAQ

How fast can my site go live?

Seven calendar days from signed agreement and content hand-off.

Do I own the domain and content?

Yes. At launch the domain, copy, and images are 100% yours.

Can I cancel the Care Plan?

Anytime, no hard feelings—just email us and we’ll hand over full admin access.

What do you need from me to start?

Logo (if you have one), yard photos (phone pics are fine), and a brief call to nail your services, hours, and pricing flow.

How do payments work?

50% deposit to book your build week, 50% on launch day. Card, ACH, or check—whatever’s easiest.

Is hosting included?

Yes. Hosting, SSL, and domain hookup are baked into the $99/mo Care Plan. Prefer to self-host? We’ll deploy to your server for a one-time $199 hand-off.

Can you add more pages later?

Sure. Extra pages are $350 each or jump to Power-Launch if you need a larger build from the start.

Will you write the copy and handle SEO?

Absolutely. We draft the text, bake in industry keywords, and fine-tune meta tags so Google knows exactly what you buy and where you are.

Do you shoot photos or video?

We can—onsite photo/video packages start at $750 in the lower 48. Most yards start with their own shots and upgrade later.
